Welcome to Forewarn!

You and your teammates are survivors of an apocalyptic world. You’ve survived this by using your basic survival skills. However, as time progresses you notice a multitude of obstacles that’ll require teamwork in order to complete. Complete these quarterly challenges by submitting your data entries, maintaining your health with a weekly login, and answering the weekly trivia! Prizes will be awarded quarterly based on points earned from the weekly login, data entry, and trivia. The annual grand prizes will be rewarded to the top three teams based on their demand forecast accuracy. View deadlines and upcoming events through the calendar.

After countless miles of traveling, your team has decided to settle in a remotely safe area. Now you must gather materials in order to build a safe shelter.
